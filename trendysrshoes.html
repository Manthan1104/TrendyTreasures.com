Product details

Step into the ISPA Link Axis, a continuation of our journey to push the needle of footwear design. "Every part can be recycled", says a designer from the ISPA team. "It represents progress over perfection—creating a product with a second life in mind". Guided by the principles of modularity and waste reduction, the design uses interlocking components, as few materials as possible and zero glue. When you've finished wearing them, drop them off at a participating Nike store to help extend their life. It also delivers a brand-new look and feel, developed with the same thoughtful approach. The underfoot sensation, highlighted by mechanical Nike Air cushioning, adds responsiveness and the perfect amount of bounce. Even the sporty colours are purposeful: they nod to the very first Link Axis prototype, now housed in a museum exhibit on the future of circular design. It's a reminder of our promise: everlasting progress towards the forever shoe.
